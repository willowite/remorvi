FAQ

Common questions

Will the photo or video look different?

No. The image itself doesn’t change by a single pixel: we only work with the service data inside the file. Quality stays untouched.

Where does the real camera data come from?

From a device profile: send your own shot and remorvi builds your camera’s profile from it. Or pick one of 30+ ready-made presets: iPhone, Samsung, Ray-Ban Meta and more.

What happens to my files?

You send a file → processing → you get it back → it’s deleted from the server. We store nothing and share nothing.

Which formats are supported?

Photo: JPEG, PNG, HEIC. Video: MP4, MOV. Send it as a file (📎 → “File”, not “Photo”) – otherwise Telegram compresses it on upload.

How much does it cost?

New users get 3 days of Unlimited free, with every feature. Then two plans: Free (1 file a day) or Unlimited at 899 ₽/mo.
